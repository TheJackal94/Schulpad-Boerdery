Our Farm-Fresh Products

A closer look at the quality produce grown at Schulpad Boerdery.

Sweet Melons

Muskmelon and Honeydew melons are cultivated all year round, so we plant new seedlings every week of the year. In summer, fruit can be harvested within 8-10 weeks from the planting date, while in winter it takes 12-14 weeks.

We continuously trial new varieties to find the best fruit for our unique conditions, focusing on taste, shelf-life, and appearance. Seeds are currently sourced from Europe and the USA.

Muskmelon Varieties

  • Summer: Caribbean Gold, Jemma
  • Winter: Touchdown, Delightful, Star 8815, AcutFresh

Honeydew Melon Varieties

  • Summer: Summer Dew, Honey Chow
  • Winter: Honey Pac

Peppers

Our climate is ideal for cultivating peppers for the local market from March through to November each year.

Field Peppers

Red and yellow peppers are planted from December to July. Their harvest time, volume, and quality are greatly influenced by weather conditions.

  • Red Peppers: Hercules
  • Yellow Peppers: Solanor

Greenhouse Peppers

To optimize volume and size during the winter season, we also plant peppers in greenhouses from April to June. This method protects the fruit from insects and harsh weather, resulting in heavy fruit with thick flesh and an excellent appearance.

  • Red Peppers: Red Jewel
  • Yellow Peppers: Sunshine
